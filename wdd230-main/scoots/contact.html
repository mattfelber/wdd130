Scoots is located on the island of Cozumel off the coast of Mexico in the western Caribbean Sea. We have two physical rental locations on Cozumel. One is near the Playa del Carmen-Cozumel Ferry dock in el Centro de Cozumel and the other is near the Terminal Puerta Maya further south along the coast which caters to the cruise lines and patrons. We promote friendly and easy to rent services of motor scooters, Jeeps, and side-by-sides ATVs.
