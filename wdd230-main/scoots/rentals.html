Rentals

Available rental options and prices.

  1. A $50 security deposit is required to confirm reservations.
  2. A valid drivers license is required in hand at time of rental.
  3. No one under 12 years of age is allowed to ride on the scooters.
  4. Fuel charge not included.
  5. Unlimited mileage.
  6. Liability insurance is free of charge.
  7. Reservations rate requires 24 hour prior notification.
  8. Free delivery to Cozumel hotel, Airbnb, or private residence for 2+ day rentals.
  9. Payment via Google Pay, cash, credit card, debit card, or PayPal.
  10. Rentals may be returned to either location.

Types of Rentals

Feel free to browse our available rental options.

Honda Metro- 49cc

European-style engineered to embody American practicality. Electric starter, underseat storage to help you on the go. Turn short rides into a metropolis of fun, save on gas.

Honda Dio 110cc

Powerful engine with optimum perfomance, superb mileage. Combined braking system. Retractable hooks make it a breeze to carry anything and everything.

Honda PCX150 149cc

V-Matic transmission, you get start-up-and-go riding without needing to shift. 2.1 gallon fuel tank, stellar fuel economy saves you money. Conquer your city in style, on the sleek and swift.

Honda Pioneer 1000

Smart technology. Superior materials. Refined engineering. Honda’s well-earned and world-famous reputation for reliability and overall quality. Everybody aboard is going to enjoy the ride.

2-door Jeep Wrangler

Stronger axle tubes, larger brakes, 4x4 systems, rugged steel skid plates and more all add up for you to take the most capable 2 door Jeep Wrangler further than you ever thought possible.

4-door Jeep Wrangler

Smart design combined with advanced technological features for a safe and secure journey. Systems include ParkSense Front and Rear Park Assist to keep you aware of your surroundings.
